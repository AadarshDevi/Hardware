# AMD Radeon RX Graphics Cards

## Examples

1. 7970
2. 7700 XT
3. 8900

## Naming Convention
1. first number (7, 8, 9) are the generation of the gpu card. higher the generation, more expensive the card.
2. last 3 numbers show the power of the gpu. higher the number, he more powerful and expensive.
3. XT/XTX are more powerful than base counterpart.
